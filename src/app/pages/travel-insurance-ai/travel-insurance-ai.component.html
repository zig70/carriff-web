Travel Insurance AI Chat

A conversational AI assistant that scans itineraries, answers coverage questions in plain English, and delivers instant quotes, all within a single chat window. Contact us to see a demo or discuss how this can transform your business.

The travel insurance industry is being reshaped by AI. Customers expect instant answers, not 40-page policy PDFs. Insurers that adopt conversational AI are seeing higher conversion rates, fewer call-centre contacts, and stronger customer satisfaction scores.

This demo shows how Carriff Digital builds insurtech AI chat solutions for travel insurance providers. The assistant reads a customer's booking confirmation, answers coverage questions with direct policy references, flags gaps like single-item baggage limits, and delivers a personalised quote, all inside a single conversation. No forms, no hold music, no drop-off.

Itinerary scanning

Plain-English policy Q&A

Customers ask coverage questions in their own words. The AI answers instantly, citing the exact policy section and page number for full transparency.

Smart baggage guidance

Instant quote & upsell

A personalised quote is generated and emailed in the same conversation. The AI then offers relevant add-ons — like an excess waiver — with a single tap.
